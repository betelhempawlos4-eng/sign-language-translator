Getting started

How SignBridge works

Up and running in under 60 seconds — no downloads, no installs.

1
Select your language
Choose your country. Each has its own independent sign language with unique handshapes and grammar.
2
Start the camera
Click Start Camera and allow access. Position your hand in good lighting with a plain background.
3
Sign steadily
Hold each sign for 1–2 seconds. AI smooths detections across 5 frames so only stable signs get added.
4
Read, speak, share
Your translation builds in real time. Tap Speak to hear it, Copy to paste it anywhere.

📖 Tips for best detection

1Good lighting: Face a window or lamp. No light behind you.
2Plain background: Helps MediaPipe track your hand clearly.
3Hold steady: Each sign needs ~1 second to register.
4Distance: Keep hand 30–60cm from camera.

🌍 About sign languages

Sign languages are complete, independent languages with their own grammar and vocabulary. ASL and BSL are mutually unintelligible despite both being from English-speaking countries. There are approximately 300 sign languages worldwide.
